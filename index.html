Course Project Introduction:

The course project we are working on aims to design a city that is sustainable and can host all the required facilities that contribute towards the development of the city along with preserving the intricate culture of the city, state, and country through tourism.

CHITRANAGARI: The City of Art

The designed city is assumed to be situated in the state of Rajasthan, India, with an area of approximately 1423 square km. The city is called Chitranagari, which translates to 'City of Art'. Just like its name, the city offers its visitors immense cultural heritage and diversity in the form of museums, forts, and palaces. It also hosts recreational sites such as amusement parks, zoological and botanical gardens. The city is well connected with roads and highways on the outskirts, supported by metro within the city. There are two government offices (North and South hub) for smooth functioning. These, along with other details, make this city a sustainable and economically driving city.
